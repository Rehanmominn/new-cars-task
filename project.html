JDM, or Japanese Domestic Market, cars refer to vehicles that are manufactured specifically for the Japanese market. These cars often have unique features, specifications, and designs tailored to Japanese regulations and consumer preferences. JDM cars are highly regarded by enthusiasts around the world for their performance, reliability, and engineering prowess.
